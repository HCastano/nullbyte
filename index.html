NullByte is a Toronto based consulting company focused on the intersection between blockchain and Rust. We've been involved with the blockchain space for over three years, and in the Rust space for about half as long. We have experience working with Ethereum, having done work at the client level. More recently we've been working with Substrate, a next-generation blockchain framework. We love open-source, remote work, and projects that aim to make the world a little better for everyone. If you're looking for help with your next Rusty blockchain project, let's get in touch.
